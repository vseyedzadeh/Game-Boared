About Us

Bored is designed to challenge different areas of your brain, but in a fun and game-like way.
Our program has fun and engaging games that challenge different areas of your brain, including: Memory, Focus, Speed of Thinking, Logic and Problem-solving, Language and Visual-Spatial Recognition and in some cases, customizes training to give you the appropriate level of challenge along the way. Bored is simple, but effective, and fun to use!
